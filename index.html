About Us

ChickenSMP is a premium Minecraft survival server dedicated to providing a clean, lag-free, and engaging environment. We focus on enhancing the vanilla experience with quality-of-life improvements, professional moderation, and a mature community that values collaboration and creativity.

Features

Enhanced Survival

Experience vanilla survival at its core, carefully balanced with subtle improvements to keep gameplay fair and exciting.

Strong Community

Join a welcoming, mature player base. Build towns, establish trade routes, and collaborate on massive projects.

Weekly Events

Participate in professionally organized server events, from build competitions to custom PvE challenges.
